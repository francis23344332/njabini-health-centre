NJABINI HEALTH CENTRE WEBSITE

Njabini Health Centre is a Ministry of Health basic health centre located in Njabani/Kiburu (Nbi-Nakuru Highway. Branch To The Left At Flyover Up To Njabini Town) Kinangop in Nyandarua County. As of 2021, the facility was fully operational with a capacity of 6 beds. It is regulated by Ministry of Health under registration number 10878. You can reach Njabini Health Centre by call or text through their number (to be updated).
