Njabini Health Centre

Njabini Health Centre is a Ministry of Health basic health centre located in Njabani/Kiburu (Nbi-Nakuru Highway. Branch To The Left At Flyover Up To Njabini Town) Kinangop in Nyandarua County. As of 2021, the facility was fully operational with a

These are the heads and their roles

    DIRECTOR OF MEDICAL SERVICES
  1. Roles and Responsibilities
  2. Undertaking general diagnosis, care treatment and rehabilitation of surgical patients;
  3. Carrying out specialized clinical care;
  4. Training, consulting and performing emergency and elective general surgical operations;
  5. Carrying out medical legal services;
  6. Undertaking health research;
  7. Analyzing medical data for reports.

    |
  • PHAMACY
  1. Roles and Responsibilities
  2. Coordinate delivery of pharmaceutical care services for both inpatient and outpatient;
  3. Management of patients/clients requiring specialized pharmaceutical services;
  4. Supervision of pharmaceutical technologists and support staff deployed in the pharmacy department;
  5. Conduct routine training to brief clinicians on updates related to pharmaceutical products and information;
  6. Coordinate pharmacovigilance activities and ensure patient safety is guaranteed;
  7. Carrying out research in clinical matters;
  8. Developing and implementing therapeutic drug monitoring plans.

  • MEDICAL OFFICERS
  1. Roles and Responsibilities
  2. Diagnose, care and treat clients;
  3. Perform medical and surgical procedures;
  4. Prepare and respond to emergencies and disasters;
  5. Participate in management of medicines, medical instruments and equipment;
  6. Provide health education;
  7. Teach and coach medical students, nursing students and clinical officer interns;
  8. Prepare clinical documents for legal and medical purposes.

    Medical Laboratory Technician
  1. Roles and Responsibilities
  2. scrutinizing laboratory requisition forms/specimens;
  3. Preparing clients for specimen collection; of the specimen;
  4. Preparation and submission of patient laboratory reports for management.
